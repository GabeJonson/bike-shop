Do you remember what it is like to be cruising on the boulevard and catching fresh spring wind on the shiny and chromed old school bike? Bikes were our founder’s love from the 2nd grade (the time when his parents bought him his first Black Flash city bike). Since that time he’s decided to open his own store that would sell everything that is connected with bikes. So, as you can see dreams come true and today we’re glad to welcome you at our Bike Store.

Our online shop is the best place to buy bikes, accessories and other related products. Perfect service, reliable shipping system and flexible discount and loyalty programs - these are the main components that have ensured the success of our business. Great team chemistry and flawless customer care will surprise you instantly and you’ll notice that you are in a right place where people know everything about bicycles.
